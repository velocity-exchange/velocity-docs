# Spot Trading

<Callout type="warning">
**Spot trading has been removed from the Velocity program.** There is no spot order book on Velocity — spot orders cannot be placed, and margin spot trading is not available. Spot markets still exist, but only as the venue for collateral and [Borrow & Lend](/protocol/borrow-lend).
</Callout>

## What you can still do with spot assets

- **Swap tokens** — spot swaps remain fully available with up to **5x leverage**. You can exchange any supported pair directly at [app.velocity.exchange/swap](https://velocity.exchange/swap). A swap settles against your balances and can draw on margin (opening a borrow against your other collateral) when you have margin trading enabled.
- **Deposit as collateral** — deposits of spot assets can act as cross-collateral for perpetuals trading, but this depends on the asset's tier: only assets in the highest-privilege tiers count toward your collateral value, while lower tiers may be deposited (e.g. to borrow against) without contributing collateral weight. Each market's current tier is shown in-app / on-chain. See [Cross Collateral Deposits](/protocol/getting-started/cross-collateral-deposits).
- **Earn and borrow** — deposits automatically earn lending yield, and you can borrow against them. See [Borrow & Lend](/protocol/borrow-lend).

## What was removed

- The spot order book (spot DLOB) and all spot order placement, including spot maker/resting orders. Any attempt to place a spot order is rejected on-chain (`SpotDlobTradingDisabled`).
- On-book margin spot trading — with no spot order book, there is nothing to margin trade on-book.

<Callout type="info">
The per-subaccount margin toggle (`is_margin_trading_enabled`) itself still exists and is **not** removed. It governs whether swaps and withdrawals may open a borrow against your collateral.
</Callout>

If you're looking for leveraged directional exposure, use [Perpetuals Trading](/protocol/trading/perpetuals-trading) instead.
