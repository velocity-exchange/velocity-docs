# Spot Trading

<Callout type="warning">
**Spot trading has been removed from the Velocity program.** There is no spot order book on Velocity — spot orders cannot be placed, and margin spot trading is not available. Spot markets still exist, but only as the venue for collateral and [Borrow & Lend](/protocol/borrow-lend).
</Callout>

## What you can still do with spot assets

- **Swap tokens** — spot swaps remain fully available. You can exchange any supported pair directly at [app.velocity.exchange/swap](https://app.velocity.exchange/swap), with up to 5x leverage.
- **Deposit as collateral** — every supported spot asset you deposit can act as cross-collateral for perpetuals trading. See [Cross Collateral Deposits](/protocol/getting-started/cross-collateral-deposits).
- **Earn and borrow** — deposits automatically earn lending yield, and you can borrow against them. See [Borrow & Lend](/protocol/borrow-lend).

## What was removed

- The spot order book (spot DLOB) and all spot order placement, including spot maker/resting orders.
- Margin spot trading (the per-subaccount spot margin toggle).

If you're looking for leveraged directional exposure, use [Perpetuals Trading](/protocol/trading/perpetuals-trading) instead.
