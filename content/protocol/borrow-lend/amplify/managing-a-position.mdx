# Managing a Position

## Monitoring your position

You can monitor the total PnL, deposit amount, net APY, and other details related to your current open position.

<img src="/assets/amplify-monitor-a-position-1.png" alt="" width="600" height="auto"></img>

You can deposit more or withdraw to change the existing position.

## Closing your position

### Reduce position size

You can reduce position size by clicking [Withdraw] and reduce the deposited
amount to your intended amount. Velocity Amplify will automatically adjust your
borrowing position to meet your intended position size.

### Unwind a position

Close a position by withdrawing 100% of the deposited amount.
