# Rewards

Velocity does not run an on-chain points or fuel program — Drift's "Fuel" points/incentives
system (per-user fuel accrual, fuel boosts, fuel seasons) was removed for Velocity and has no
on-chain replacement.

The protocol-level reward mechanism that does survive from Drift, in changed form, is the
[referral program](/protocol/rewards/referral-links): referrers earn a share of their referred
users' taker fees, paid out through an escrow account rather than the legacy epoch-capped path.

<Callout type="info">
Velocity or its ecosystem partners may run time-limited incentive campaigns (for example,
deposit or collateral incentives funded by a partner protocol) from time to time. Any such
campaigns will be announced separately and reflected in the app — none are documented here
because none are currently active or verified as ongoing Velocity programs.
</Callout>
